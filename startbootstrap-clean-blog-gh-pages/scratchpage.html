Why Turtles Are Cool

Turtles are fascinating ancient animals. Master Oogway from the Disney movie "Kung Fu Panda" is a prime example of how turtles are often portrayed to be wise and knowledgeable animals. He is the mentor to a group of powerful warriors who honor and respect him. Just like they respect him, we should respect turtles.

While turtles cannot directly communicate with us, they are still very cool. Some people belive that keeping a turtle in your home is a token of good luck and fortune (I'm some people). Excellent movies aside, turtles are an excellent housemate for people of all ages. There are many benefits to keeping turtles as a pet, especially when compared to other popular pets like cats and dogs. Here is why you should get a pet turtle:

  1. Turtles are low-maintenence
      They eat common, inexpensive food that does not need a lot of preparation
      They can live on their own and do not require interaction from other animals or people to be happy. Most turtles are pretty solitary.


  2. Watching a turtle can be calming
      Turtles engage in a lot of repetitive actions that can be very soothing to watch (#science)
      While I personally have never seen one used as a therapy animal, turtles can help lower heart rate and blood pressure. They can also have a positive effect on individuals with mental health disorders.


  3. They live in a tank or aquarium
      Turtle tanks are not only a good habitat for a turtle, but also serve as decoration for a room
      A tank is a contained space that keeps the turtle in one relatively small spot. Unlike other pets, you will always know where your turtle is.
Source: turtlean.com
